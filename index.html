About NoorNetwork Community Database

Welcome to NoorNetwork, the comprehensive solution for managing community outreach and engagement. Our platform is meticulously designed to streamline the process of collecting and organizing data about community visits, events, and interactions. With NoorNetwork, you can effortlessly add and track data, visualize community engagement on a map, and schedule visits to ensure continuous support and communication within the community.

Our intuitive interface makes it easy for users to enter data, update records, and get a geographical view of the community landscape. Whether you're a community leader, a volunteer, or a member looking to contribute, NoorNetwork is your go-to tool for fostering growth and connection.

This program was developed by Noorullah Khan in 2024 to help manage the outreach and engagement efforts in the Muslim Community. It aims to empower communities to better connect with their members, track engagement efforts, and plan future outreach.

Key Features:

    Data Entry: Securely add detailed information about community members and locations.

    Geographic Mapping: View community data on an interactive map for easy location visualization.

    Visit Tracking: Keep a log of visits to community members, including notes and follow-up actions.

    Accessibility: Designed for simplicity, ensuring that all users can navigate and utilize the platform with ease.

How to Use NoorNetwork:

Here are simple steps to get started:

    Adding Data: Select "Add Data" from the main menu and submit the form with community member details.

    Adding a Visit: Use "Add Visit" to log new visits, or choose a pin on the map and select "Add Visit".

    Viewing the Map: Click on "View Map" to see the interactive map and access detailed information by choosing a pin. Click "Refresh Visits" to update the map with the latest data.
